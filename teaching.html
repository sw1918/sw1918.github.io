Directed Research Groups (DRG)

HCI, Journalism & Misinformation: Understanding how journalists process, analyze and make sense of data

Co-facilitated with Melinda McClure Haughey and overseen by Dr. Kate Starbird. 4/2019-Ongoing.

Students of this DRG are guided in research activities aimed at understanding and aiding journalists' work when using social media data. These include interviewing local journalists, analyzing data, reading current literature, and designing tools to help journalists deal with the vast amount of data in today's social media landscape.
